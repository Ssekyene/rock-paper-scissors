ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS

Open the console (in the developers tools) eg (CTRL + SHIFT + J) for chrome browser and then click on the button below to play!

Each turn of play has a maximum of 5 rounds.

The game will end before finishing all rounds when one of the players is left with few rounds to make them either a win or a draw. eg When one hits 3 wins in 3 rounds, the other is left with 2 rounds and therefore has no chance to make it.

  • Win - 1 point
  • Lose - no point
  • Draw - no point for each

Note: When you enter something wrong you replay the round!

To play more rounds, click the reset button and then the play button.
